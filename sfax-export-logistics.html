What I do

Everything between your factory and your buyer

I handle the full export chain — from first document to final delivery confirmation — so nothing falls through the cracks.

Document preparation

Commercial invoices, packing lists, certificates of origin, EUR1 certificates, and phytosanitary documents — all prepared correctly the first time.

Carrier booking

I compare rates across Aramex, DHL, and local freight forwarders and book the best option for your shipment size, destination, and timeline.

Customs coordination

I work with your customs broker and verify that all declarations match your documents — avoiding delays, fines, and returned shipments.

Shipment tracking

You receive status updates at each stage — pickup, departure, customs clearance, delivery — without having to chase anyone yourself.

EU compliance

Exporting to Europe? I verify HS codes, sanitary certificates, and buyer-specific requirements so your goods clear EU customs without issues.

Cost negotiation

I negotiate freight rates on your behalf. Pooling shipments across clients gives you volume pricing you couldn't get alone.
